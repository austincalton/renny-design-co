Our Process

How we work with you

The General Flow

  • Initial Consultation (~ 1 hour): This is where we chat to get a better understanding of your needs, the timeline you have in mind, and the budget that you have to work with.
  • Proposal Generation: From the information gathered during our consultation, we draft an outline that covers goals to be completed, the timeline for deliverables, and the project's cost.
  • Client Review: This is your chance to review our proposal. If everything looks good to you, you'll sign off and we'll begin our work. If not, we make changes accordingly and move on once you are fully satisfied.
  • Working Phase: At this point, we are officially working towards your goal. During this time we submit any deliverables to you as requested, undertake brief meetings to provide status updates, and incorporate your feedback as we progress.
  • Review Phase: During this time, we review our final deliverables with you. You let us know if there are any final edits to be made and we will adjust accordingly within the framework of our proposal. If you require additional work to be done outside the proposal, we'll draft an extension and move through our process accordingly.
  • Final Exchange: You get the "keys" to your new website + graphics! We turn over all materials that have been created for you and answer any operational quesrtions you may have.
